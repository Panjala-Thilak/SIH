About Us



This Students Grievance support system is user friendly and it is also an easy access web application. It is accessible for students, members of student Grievance Redressal Committees, respective heads . This application maintains a direct contact between students and student Grievance Redressal Committees.

Our website would initially have a welcome page of login for students as well as committee people . By this, students turn on to new page where they have different options to select a level to which their complaint belong to .There they can post their problem/complaint . This complaint would reach the respective person and solved soon . All the records are maintained for all complaints sorted by solved and unsolved . Any delays in solving these would be noticed by the head of committee and valid actions are taken .
